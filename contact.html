Frequently Asked Questions

My key isn't lighting up — is that a bug?

Not necessarily. Some keys (like Fn, the Windows key, and certain media controls) are intercepted by your operating system before reaching the browser — no web-based tester can detect them. If a regular alphanumeric or punctuation key isn't registering, that's worth reporting. Please include your browser name, OS, and which key is affected.

How do I report a bug effectively?

The more detail, the better. Please include: (1) which tool/page has the bug, (2) your browser and version (e.g. Chrome 124), (3) your operating system (Windows 11, macOS Sonoma, etc.), (4) your keyboard layout if relevant, and (5) steps to reproduce the problem. Screenshots are a huge help if you can attach them via email.

Can I suggest a new keyboard tool?

Absolutely — we love hearing ideas from the community. Use the contact form above and select "Feature Request" as the subject. Describe what the tool would do and why it would be useful. No promises, but popular ideas often make it into the roadmap.

Do you have an email address I can write to directly?

The contact form above is the fastest way to reach us, as it routes to the right team member based on your topic. For direct correspondence, replies will come from our team email and you can continue the conversation there.
